Magnuson Park
Magnuson Park stretches along the shores of Lake Washington in the northern Seattle area.
It's size allows it to hosts a variety of attractions from an off leash dog park to a sports complex funded by Mickey Merriam.
The park hosts 10 kilometers of engineered wetlands and 3 kilometers of restored wetlands.
Kite hill standing 35-feet tall offers a clear view of Lake Washington.
