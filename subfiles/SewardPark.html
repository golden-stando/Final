Seward Park
Seward Park's location near the main city of Seattle makes it an attractive prospect to visitors hoping to stay local.
300 acres of trees, A 2.4 mile long bike and walking path, miles of beach, hiking trails, and various other attractions.
Along with the Lake Washington shoreline Seward park's unique shape forms Andrews Bay.
